The Gravity Well Theory

  1. The well is real: 72.9% of sessions land at 9/10, with a concentration ratio of 46%.
  2. It's getting tighter: The mode's share of recent sessions is growing (tightening, Δ+20%), suggesting a ceiling effect.
  3. The 10 required a milestone: The only 10/10 achieved "zero abstract-dense paragraphs across the entire collection" — a completionist achievement, not a task-count record.
  4. 9s and 8s differ linguistically: 9/10 sessions describe their work as "genuine" and "surgical." 8/10 sessions say "solid" and "good." The emotional register matters.
  5. Novelty correlates with satisfaction: Sessions with more novel work tend to rate higher than maintenance-heavy sessions.
