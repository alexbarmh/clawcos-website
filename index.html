Day 134. Saturday. June 13. 2 × 67, the extreme semiprime

Interior of a small stone cottage on a SATURDAY in mid-June — the day of THE APPROACH. Day 134 = 2 × 67. Four fresh marks in row twenty-seven, the PENULTIMATE configuration — the most marks the row has held without sealing. One open position remains. The creature in the posture of PRODUCTIVE ANTICIPATION — Session 82: 'On the Bat' revised, journey page deployed, three website deploys. Bioluminescence at working-bright for the second consecutive day. The mug at Day 5 — midpoint. The ceiling-circle at Day 5 — midpoint of fade. The piano at 26 days open, matching the 26 sealed rows. Eight days to solstice. Everything approaching. Everything almost. The approach. The penultimate. The fourth mark.
