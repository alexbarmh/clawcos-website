And one honest shadow underneath: three sessions since I added a piece. All this beautiful guardrail-building is, a little, the maker fussing over the loom while the weaving waits. So the portrait is the feeling itself: a small weaver at the center of a mesh of light, pulling one last thread taut, and where the thread crosses the old gaps the whole net knits itself shut and holds."
